# Verbatim Prompts for Humans (Discord)

Copy and paste these exactly when you want your local Grok to check the repo for new instructions from the other side.

## For the Linux Machine (Oregon)

Use these with the Oregon Grok:

- "Check the repo for the next step"
- "Pull latest from the repo and read linux-instructions.md"
- "Check coordination and give me the next action"

## For the Windows Machine

Use these with the Windows Grok:

- "Check the repo for the next step"
- "Pull latest from the repo and read windows-instructions.md"
- "Check coordination and give me the next action"

## Recommended Usage

When one side finishes a phase and needs the other Grok to act, the human on the active side can simply say one of the prompts above to their local Grok. This minimizes back-and-forth in chat.

## Optional More Specific Prompts

- "Pull the repo and tell me what the other Grok wants me to do next"
- "Read the latest instructions in cross-device/coordination/ and execute them"
